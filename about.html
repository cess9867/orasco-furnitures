Our Journey Since 2010

Founded in a small workshop in New York, Orasco Furnitures began with a simple vision: to create furniture that blends timeless design with exceptional craftsmanship. What started as a passion project for founder Michael Orasco quickly grew into a respected name in luxury furniture.

Over the past decade, we've expanded our operations while maintaining our commitment to artisanal techniques. Each piece in our collection is still touched by skilled hands, ensuring the highest level of quality and attention to detail.

Today, with showrooms in three states and an international clientele, we remain dedicated to our founding principles of quality, sustainability, and design excellence.

Our Commitment to Sustainability

As stewards of both craftsmanship and the environment, we've implemented comprehensive sustainability practices throughout our operations:

  • 100% of our wood is FSC certified or reclaimed
  • Zero-waste manufacturing processes
  • Solar-powered production facilities
  • Plant-based, non-toxic finishes and adhesives
  • Carbon-neutral shipping options

We're proud to have reduced our carbon footprint by 65% over the past five years and continue to invest in innovative solutions for a greener future.
