Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about bulk orders

What is the minimum order quantity for bulk pricing?

Our bulk pricing starts at 10+ pieces of the same item or a mixed order totaling 20+ pieces. For custom projects, we can discuss minimums based on your specific requirements.

How long does it take to fulfill a bulk order?

Production times vary based on the complexity and size of the order. Standard bulk orders typically take 8-12 weeks from approval to delivery. Custom designs may require 12-16 weeks. We provide detailed timelines during the quoting process.

Do you offer design services for commercial spaces?

Yes, we provide complimentary design consultation for all bulk orders. Our design team will work with you to create cohesive furniture solutions that match your brand identity and space requirements.

What shipping and installation services do you provide?

We offer nationwide shipping and professional installation services. For large projects, we coordinate phased deliveries and provide on-site installation teams to ensure efficient setup with minimal disruption to your operations.

What warranty do you offer for commercial furniture?

All our commercial furniture comes with a 5-year structural warranty and a 3-year fabric/finish warranty. For hospitality and healthcare applications, we offer extended warranty options.
