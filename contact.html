Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services

How long does delivery take?

Standard delivery takes 5-7 business days within the continental US. For custom pieces, delivery times vary based on the complexity of the order, typically ranging from 4-8 weeks. You'll receive a detailed timeline when placing your order.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping costs and delivery times vary based on destination. Please contact our customer service team for specific rates and timelines for your location.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy on standard items. Custom pieces are non-returnable unless defective. Returns are subject to a 15% restocking fee, and customers are responsible for return shipping costs. All returns must be in original condition with packaging.

Can I visit your showroom?

Absolutely! Our flagship showroom at 123 Design Avenue, New York is open Monday-Friday 9am-6pm and Saturday 10am-4pm. We recommend scheduling an appointment for personalized design consultations, especially for custom projects.

How does the custom design process work?

Our custom design process begins with a consultation (in-person or virtual) to understand your vision and requirements. Our designers then create concept sketches and 3D renderings. Once approved, we provide a detailed quote and timeline. Production begins after a 50% deposit, with the balance due before delivery.
